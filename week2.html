A Tribute to Dr Myles Munroe

"The man that was more famous than his country President"

Biography

Myles Munroe, (20 April 1954-9 November 2014) was a Bahamian evangelist and ordained minister avid professor of the Kingdom of God, author, speaker and leadership consultant who founded and led the Bahamas Faith Ministries International (BFMI) and Myles Munroe International (MMI). He was chief executive officer and chairman of the board of the International Third World Leaders Association and president of the International Leadership Training Institute as well as the author of numerous books.

The following are notable things about Dr. Myles Munroe:

  1. He received his Bachelor of Fine Arts, Education, and Theology in 1978
  2. He received his Masters degree in administration from the University of Tulsa in 1980
  3. Dr. Myles Munroe served as an adjunct professor of the Graduate School of Theology at the Oral ROberts University
  4. Following his graduation from the University of Tulsa, Munroe founded Bahamas Faith Ministries International in the early 1980s
  5. He got married in 1978
  6. His wife, Ruth Munroe, served as co-pastor with him at BFMI.
  7. Munroe was the author and coauthor of numerous books and Biblical-related study guides
  8. He featured as a speaker on motivational and Bible-study recordings
  9. Munrie and his wife had two children, Myles, Jr. (known as Chairo), and a daughter, Charisa
  10. He was a major economic adviser to several Presidents around the world
  11. Munroe gave a powerful sermon on relationship in Faith Tabernacle Ota, Living Faith Church
  12. Sadly, Munroe and his wife died in a private plane crash during airport approach on 9 November 2014
